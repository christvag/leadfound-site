How It Works

From Target List to Submitted Form in Minutes

Four simple steps to automate your entire outreach pipeline through website contact forms.

1

Upload Your Leads

Import a CSV of target websites or let our crawler build your list from any niche, industry, or location.

2

Our agent scans each site, identifies contact, inquiry, and quote-request forms automatically with intelligent field mapping.

3

Personalize Messages

Submit & Track

Forms are submitted at human-like intervals. Track success rates, failures, and responses in real time from your dashboard.
